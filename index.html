Daycare

We provide a safe and nurturing environment for young children to learn and play during the day. These facilities are staffed by trained professionals who are responsible for the care and education of the children.

Nursery

At our Nursery we provide a safe and stimulating environment for very young children to develop their social, emotional, physical and cognitive skills.

Extra Class

Extra classes we provide are additional educational opportunities for students beyond the regular curriculum. These classes may include subjects such as music, art, dance, languages, or other enrichment activities.

Day Camp

At our Day camp we provide children with fun and engaging activities during school breaks or summer vacation. These facilities typically include a wide range of activities such as arts and crafts, sports, games, and educational programs that are designed to keep children entertained and active.

Why choose us?

We Care for Everything

Interactive Learning

Children learn best when they are actively engaged in the learning process, and we strive to provide them the oppertunities which allow children to explore, discover, and create,fostering their curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking skills.

Our Library

Our library facility helps children develop a love of reading and improve their literacy skills. Wehave a variety of books that cater to different reading levels, interests and learning styles, which means every child can find something exciting that they will enjoy reading.

Art and Craft

Our art and craft activities are designed to help children develop creativity, critical thinking skills, and problem-solving abilities.From painting, drawing, sculpting, collage and even textile arts, our art program is designed to provide children with a rich and diverse experience.

Gym

The modern gym we have provides children with an opportunity to engage in physical activity, develop gross motor skills, and socialize with their peers. Our gym is specially designed for young children with age-appropriate equipment and activities that are safe and fun for them.
